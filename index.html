Deities is a high-end concept store for designer fashion and homeware. With the name, I immediately saw symmetry and explored its notion in the context of religion and nature. I stumbled upon the Architecture of Sound by Tanya Harris and was inspired by both the architectural quality and beauty of the church floorplans she referenced, and the intricate mosaic patterns of mosques. This sacred geometry found in religious architecture has rootsin the study of nature, being seen from the spiral shaped growth of sea shells to our DNA structure.
